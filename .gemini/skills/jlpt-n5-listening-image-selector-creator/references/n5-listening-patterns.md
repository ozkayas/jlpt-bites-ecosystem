# JLPT N5 Listening (Mondai 1) Logic Patterns

To create authentic N5 listening questions, use one of these 4 primary distractor patterns found in real exams:

## 1. The Reconsideration (Vazgeçme)
- **Concept:** Speakers discuss an option, agree on it, then one speaker raises a concern, leading to a final different choice.
- **Logic:** Option A -> Option B? -> Concern -> Final Option C.
- **Example:** "Let's buy a cake." -> "Is it too small?" -> "Yes, let's get cookies instead." -> "Actually, let's buy a sandwich."

## 2. The Shortage (Eksik Tamamlama)
- **Concept:** Speakers list what they already have and identify what is missing. The question asks what they need to GET/BUY.
- **Logic:** Have A, Have B, No C -> Buy C.
- **Example:** "We have bread and juice. We only need butter." -> Correct Option: Butter.

## 3. The Attribute Filter (Nitelik Eleme)
- **Concept:** Multiple similar objects are discussed, but only one fits all criteria (size, color, pattern, quantity).
- **Logic:** Size (Small/Large) + Pattern (Fish/Flower).
- **Example:** "The fish plate is nice but small. The flower one is large. Let's take the large one." -> Correct: Large Flower Plate.

## 4. The Negative Condition (Unutma/Hata)
- **Concept:** A speaker mentions a condition that *should* be there but isn't, or an action that was *not* taken.
- **Logic:** "I forgot X" or "I didn't do Y".
- **Example:** "It was sunny but I forgot my hat." -> Correct: Person without a hat.
